Connecting With a Team

Connecting with a strong team of people is the first step of your sports journey. Here are some top resources you can use to create or join a team. (All titles are clickable links)

  • Teamsnap

    Teamsnap is a site and mobile app that is especially useful for coaches and team managers. Use it to manage your team roster, schedule, etc.

  • LeagueLineup

    LeagueLineup is a site that is extremely useful in finding youth and amateur sports clubs in your area, so that you can join.

  • Zogsports

    Zogsports is another very useful tool for finding yourself a team or league to join. It is very streamlined and easy to use.

  • Local Parks & Rec

    Another very good idea is to check your local area's Parks and Recreation Departement, because more than often these administrations sponsor and run sports leagues of thier own.
